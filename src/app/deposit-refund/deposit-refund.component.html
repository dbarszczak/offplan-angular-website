Many Purchasers believed, that if the developer entered into liquidation, or was forced into Bankruptcy, they would have no chance of reclaiming their lost deposits.

This is simply not the case. We specialize in reclaiming deposits back for our clients.

Our dedicated team of Lawyers, whose offices are based on THE COSTA DEL SOL, have been exceptionally successful in reclaiming clients lost deposits.

Our Lawyers will diligently pursue your claim through the Spanish Courts to reclaim your lost deposits.
